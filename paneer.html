Paneer Gravy Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Water as required

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and the oil starts separating.
  5. Once the mixture is ready, add paneer cubes and stir gently.
  6. Add water to adjust the gravy consistency and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
