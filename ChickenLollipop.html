Chicken lollipop Recipe

Chicken lollipop is a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer where a frenched chicken drumette is marinated and then batter fried or baked until crisp.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 2 hours
  • Preparation: 1 hours
  • Cooking: 1 hours

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Salt
  • Spices(like salt,pepper,garlic peste,lemon pepper)
  • Cornstarch or cornflour
  • Oil for frying
  • Other ingredents:Vinegar,soy sauce,black pepper,red chilli paste,etc.

Instructions

  1. Marinat the Chicken lollipop: Heat oil in a pan or skillet for deep frying the lollipops.
  2. Fry the Chicken Lollipop:Drop the battered lollipops one by one in hot oil, to not crowd the oil.
  3. Frying proccess: Do not move for a minute.Keep frying for another 4-5 mins on the medium heat till the chicken is cooked.
  4. Fold and serve: Fry the rest in batches.Remove onto plate and repeat the same with the balance.
  5. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
